W-9 Forms

Two separate W-9 submissions are required — one to MTA, one to SEA. They go through different channels.

📄 IRS W-9 (Official)
The official IRS form. Download, complete, and submit to MTA via their Adobe Sign link when it arrives. Most delegates select Individual / Sole proprietor and use their SSN.

Reference
Key Terms

Tap any underlined term throughout the page for a quick definition, or find the full list here.

Attendance Sheet
Paper sign-in sheet circulated during Annual Meeting sessions. You must sign it to receive your food stipend for that day. One sheet per day.
Voting Receipt
Receipt generated when you vote during the Annual Meeting. Must be turned in to the SEA table — confirms you participated in business, not just attended.
Food Stipend
SEA reimbursement for meals: breakfast $23, lunch $26, dinner $38, incidentals $6.25/day. Total: $184 over two days ($92/day). Itemized receipts required. No alcohol. No credit card statements.
Mileage Reimbursement
Calculated at $0.70/mile from your departure address to Hynes Convention Center, Boston. Only delegates who traveled to Boston are eligible. Treasurer's office calculates the dollar amount — you enter miles driven.
Pre-Convention Meeting
MTA + SEA
Required briefings before the Annual Meeting. MTA's: April 14 or 15, 4:30–7:30 PM (choose one). SEA's: April 27, 4:15 PM, in person + Zoom. Attending both is required to be eligible for the full stipend.
Statement of Intent
SOI · NEA-RA only
NEA-RA delegates only — not required for the Annual Meeting. Short form submitted to MTA confirming you'll serve as an NEA-RA delegate. Required before MTA releases any NEA-RA funds. Paired with W-9 — both must be on file.
IRS Form W-9
Federal tax form certifying your taxpayer ID (SSN for most individuals). Submitted twice: once to MTA via Adobe Sign (for NEA-RA funding), and once to SEA via email attachment (for SEA reimbursement). Contains sensitive information — handle accordingly.
Expense Voucher
NEA-RA
Form submitted to MTA Governance after the NEA-RA along with original receipts. Required to trigger post-RA reimbursement. MTA sends it in the delegate information packet.
NEA-RA Delegate Funding
MTA funding for NEA-RA travel. Local delegates: up to $800, all paid post-RA. Requires attending ≥6 of 8 sessions (75%) and ≥1 session per day (July 4–7). Missing the threshold forfeits the payment.
SEA Budget Line 60701
SEA's internal allocation for the Annual Meeting: $10,000 for hotel + $10,062.50 for food and mileage. Separate from MTA's NEA-RA delegate funding.
